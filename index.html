Band Bio

In This Moment is an American heavy metal band from Los Angeles, California, formed by singer Maria Brink and guitarist Chris Howorth in 2005. They found drummer Jeff Fabb and started the band as Dying Star. ... In 2005, bassist Newell left the band and was replaced by Jesse Landry.
